My Journey

Just completed my BSc in Software Engineering at Babcock University, I've been on an exciting journey of turning ideas into reality through code. I've gained hands-on experience as a Backend Developer at ACAD AI and Part-time Software Developer at Webswrap.

What drives me? Building tools that solve real problems. I believe software should be more than just functional—it should be intuitive, reliable, and genuinely useful. Whether it's a blockchain voting system or an AI-powered academic tool, I pour my passion into every project.
